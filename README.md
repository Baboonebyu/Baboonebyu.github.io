### 4x4 Tic-Tac-Toe
[Here is a link to the repository for the project](https://github.com/Baboonebyu/4x4-tic-tac-toe-freecode)\
\
A little bit of background on the idea for this project. I was first introduced to 4x4 tic-tac-toe at my grandma's house. In her front room she had a fabric mat on a table that could be used to play chess or checkers. On the backside of it was a 3x3 tic-tac-toe board along with a 4x4 tic-tac-toe board. I had never seen this before so my cousins and I learned the rules and started playing it. It was far more interesting than a traditional game of tic-tac-toe, with more in-depth strategy and many more win conditions. I tried to implement the game from memory. I hope you enjoy this new way of playing tic-tac-toe.
